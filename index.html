♑ CAPRICORN SEASON OVERVIEW

Dates: December 21 - January 19

Element: Cardinal Earth

Ruling Planet: Saturn

Tarot Card: The Devil (XV)

Theme: Ambition, Structure, Manifestation

Capricorn season initiates winter with grounded, practical energy. This is the time of building foundations, setting ambitious goals, and working steadily toward material mastery. Under Saturn's disciplined influence, we learn the value of patience, responsibility, and strategic planning.

📁transits/
☉dec_21_winter_solstice.dat

Winter Solstice - Sun Enters Capricorn - December 21, 2025 (15:03 UTC)

The darkest day of the year marks the beginning of Capricorn season. From this point forward, the light returns incrementally as we ascend Capricorn's mountain. This is a powerful time for setting intentions around achievement, legacy, and building lasting structures in your life.

Themes: Rebirth through darkness, finding light within limitation, honoring the longest night before the return of the sun.

Tarot: 4 of Pentacles - Conserving resources through winter; mastering material security.

♀dec_24_venus_enters_capricorn.dat

Venus Enters Capricorn - December 24, 2025 (16:26 UTC)

Love becomes practical, committed, and mature under Venus in Capricorn. This transit values traditional expressions of affection, long-term partnerships, and showing love through actions rather than words. Relationships deepen through shared goals and mutual respect.

Best For: Committing to relationships, establishing financial stability, creating lasting beauty through craftsmanship.

Tarot: 3 of Pentacles - Mastery through collaboration, building lasting works of beauty and value together.

☿jan_01_mercury_enters_capricorn.dat

Mercury Enters Capricorn - January 1, 2026 (21:11 UTC)

Start the new year with Mercury's strategic mind in ambitious Capricorn. Communication becomes more structured, focused, and goal-oriented. This is excellent for business planning, setting concrete intentions, and thinking about long-term projects with discipline and clarity.

☽jan_03_full_moon_cancer.dat

Full Moon in Cancer (13°01') - January 3, 2026 (10:02 UTC)

The first Full Moon of 2026 illuminates Cancer's domain of home, family, and emotional security. This lunation brings culmination to matters of nurturing, belonging, and creating safe emotional spaces. Release what no longer feels like home—whether that's relationships, living situations, or outdated emotional patterns.

Tarot: 4 of Cups - Emotional reassessment; contemplating what truly nourishes your soul.

♀jan_17_venus_enters_aquarius.dat

Love becomes experimental, unconventional, and freedom-oriented under Venus in Aquarius. This transit favors friendships that turn romantic, intellectual connections, and relationships that honor individual autonomy. Attraction to the unique, the eccentric, and the innovative intensifies.

Tarot: 5 of Swords - Intellectual independence in love; choosing authentic connection over conformity.

🃏tarot_devil.cfg

The Devil (XV) - Tarot of Capricorn

Keywords: Material attachments, shadow work, bondage and liberation, earthly power

The Devil card represents Capricorn's shadow—the danger of becoming enslaved to material ambition, power, or status. Yet this card also teaches that our chains are often self-imposed, and true mastery comes from recognizing and integrating our shadow nature.

Lessons: Question what controls you. Examine your relationship to power, money, and achievement. True freedom comes not from denying desire but from conscious relationship with it.

♒ AQUARIUS SEASON OVERVIEW

Dates: January 20 - February 17

Element: Fixed Air

Ruling Planet: Uranus (modern), Saturn (traditional)

Tarot Card: The Star (XVII)

Theme: Innovation, Community, Liberation

Aquarius season brings revolutionary energy, innovative thinking, and a focus on collective progress. This is the time to break free from outdated structures, embrace your unique individuality, and contribute to the evolution of humanity. Under Uranus's electric influence, expect sudden insights and quantum leaps in consciousness.

♓ PISCES SEASON OVERVIEW

Dates: February 18 - March 19

Element: Mutable Water

Ruling Planet: Neptune (modern), Jupiter (traditional)

Tarot Card: The Moon (XVIII)

Theme: Dissolution, Compassion, Transcendence

Pisces season marks the end of the astrological year, a time of spiritual completion and surrender. The boundaries between self and other, material and spiritual, begin to dissolve. Under Neptune's mystical influence, we access higher consciousness, deep compassion, and the wisdom that comes from releasing control.
